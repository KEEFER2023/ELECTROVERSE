1.1. Introduction to Magnetism

Magnetism arises from moving charges and intrinsic magnetic moments of elementary particles. In electrical engineering, it underpins transformers, motors, inductors, and communication systems.

Magnetic effects are fundamentally described using the magnetic field vector B, measured in tesla (T).
